# Objectives of Pickleball MatchForge

## Objectives

1. Serve as a smart matchmaking platform that pairs pickleball players based on skill level, availability, and play preferences.
2. Enable fast and efficient creation of regular games by automatically grouping players into balanced matches.
3. Provide flexible matching modes such as casual pairing, ranked pairing, and rotation-based play.
4. Reduce dependency on manual coordination by automating player matching and scheduling.
5. Track player performance and use it to improve future matchmaking accuracy.
6. Extend matchmaking into structured formats by supporting tournament brackets and competitive play.
7. Maintain a history of matches, pairings, and outcomes for analysis and ranking.
8. Provide organizers with tools to manage player pools, matches, and game flow.
9. Allow scalability into real-time matchmaking, notifications, and live match updates.
10. Establish a robust foundation for a full pickleball ecosystem centered around intelligent pairing.
