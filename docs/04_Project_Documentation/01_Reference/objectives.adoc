= Objectives of Pickleball MatchForge
:toc:
:toclevels: 2

== Objectives

. Serve as a smart matchmaking platform that pairs pickleball players based on skill level, availability, and play preferences.
. Enable fast and efficient creation of regular games by automatically grouping players into balanced matches.
. Provide flexible matching modes such as casual pairing, ranked pairing, and rotation-based play.
. Reduce dependency on manual coordination by automating player matching and scheduling.
. Track player performance and use it to improve future matchmaking accuracy.
. Extend matchmaking into structured formats by supporting tournament brackets and competitive play.
. Maintain a history of matches, pairings, and outcomes for analysis and ranking.
. Provide organizers with tools to manage player pools, matches, and game flow.
. Allow scalability into real-time matchmaking, notifications, and live match updates.
. Establish a robust foundation for a full pickleball ecosystem centered around intelligent pairing.
